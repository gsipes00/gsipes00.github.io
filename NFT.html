Cardano Peppers!

I love hot peppers...and the Cardano community is fired up about NFTs. So, I've decided to join the fun and the madness and mint some collectables.
Corresponding to each season: Summer, Fall, Winter, and Spring, each collectable will be designed with a witty Cardano theme.

What are NFTs? NFTs are Non-Fungible Tokens. Essentially, they are collectables, art, or any other non-fungible asset which are minted on the blockchain to legitimize uniqueness, ownership, and peer-to-peer transfer therof. When purchasing NFTs, it is always important to check that the policy ID in the marketplace matches the policy ID advertised by the artist, so that you know you are purchasing the real deal.
