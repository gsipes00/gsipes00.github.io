Corresponding to each season of Summer, Fall, Winter and Spring each collectable will be designed to a witty Cardano theme.

What are NFTs? Non-Fungible Tokens. Essentially they are collectables, art, and any other non-fungible asset which are minted on the blockchain to legitimize uniqueness, ownership, and peer to peer transfer therof. When purchasing NFTs always check that the policy ID in the marketplace matches the policy ID advertised by the artist, so that you know you are purchasing the real deal.
